About Me

I am a Psychologist and Corporate Trainer with a focus on Adult Learning and Group Dynamics My expertise lies in facilitating structured training sessions that don't just "teach" but actively change behavior.

With a background in Clinical Psychology (M.A.), I design learning journeys based on deep behavioral insights. Whether delivering workshops in metro cities or grassroots training in Jhunjhunu and Raebareli, I adapt my facilitation style to maximize engagement across diverse learner groups.

Beyond the training room, I bring the discipline of a State-Level Karate Gold Medalist and the team spirit of a University Football Goalkeeper. I believe in high energy, clear communication, and measurable progress.
